Helping you gain a mobile advantage

You probably already have aspirations for growing your enterprise with more powerful mobile capabilities. You hear about mobile all the time: faster and easier transactions for customers, the power of mobile social networking, plus improved internal systems and processes for employees and partners with realtime access to data. Then come the concerns: Can we guarantee a superior customer experience? Is there a security risk if we allow employees to "bring your own device" and they access our system? Will costs get out of control?

To help you reach your goals — cost-effectively and with strict controls — IBM offers you a comprehensive set of mobile products and services to help increase efficiencies and gain a competitive advantage.

  • Build and connect

    Increase your enterprise's agility with new, mobile apps connected to back-end systems and applications through your mobile network. Benefit from accelerated delivery and support for native and offline apps, and rapid support for iOS6. Bridge the gap between increasingly diverse mobile devices, and your enterprise data and services. Unleash the full power of mobile cloud computing solutions that extend your network applications and develop the capabilities that maximize productivity — including enterprise and app store linkage capabilities — and deliver more satisfying customer engagements.

  • IBM Mobile Foundation — Enhanced Brings together key mobile capabilities into a single integrated package that addresses the full array of challenges and opportunities. IBM Mobile Foundation delivers a range of application development, connectivity and management capabilities that support a wide variety of mobile devices and mobile app types. Bundle includes IBM Worklight, IBM WebSphere Cast Iron, and IBM Endpoint Manager for Mobile Devices.
    • IBM Worklight — Enhanced An open, mobile application platform for smartphones and tablets that helps organizations to efficiently develop, run and manage HTML5, hybrid and native applications.
    • IBM WebSphere Cast Iron An integration framework that enables companies to rapidly connect their hybrid world of public clouds, private clouds, and on-premise applications.
  • IBM Collaborative Lifecycle Management Improve development team productivity with application lifecycle management capabilities integrated with Worklight.
  • IBM Web Experience Factory Accelerate the development of multichannel applications for desktop, mobile Web and hybrid.
  • IBM WebSphere MQ The universal messaging backbone, enabling rapid, reliable, and secure transport of messages and data between applications, systems, services and extended devices.

  • Manage and secure

    Enhance business productivity as you manage and secure mobile devices, data, applications and services throughout their lifecycles. This is a critical challenge, since "bring your own device" (BYOD) policies could add an estimated 200 million mobile users to the workforce. Your IT team needs to secure transactions from virtually any device and any location, while keeping your network accessible, safe and efficient, including context-based security enabled for cloud and mobile. Stronger mobile device management (MDM) software and services means a stronger enterprise.

  • IBM Endpoint Manager for Mobile Devices — Enhanced Unify the management and security of your mobile devices and traditional endpoints while integrating them into existing IT infrastructure tools such as service desk, asset management, and security event management.
  • IBM Security Access Manager Single sign-on (SSO) solution authorizes and authenticates user access to Web and other hosted applications.
  • IBM Security Access Manager for Cloud and Mobile — New Single Sign On (SSO) capability, context-aware, risk-based access security and policy compliance.
  • IBM Security AppScan Application security testing and risk management solutions.
  • QRadar Security intelligence for the mobile enterprise — visibility, reporting and threat detection.
  • IBM Emptoris Rivermine Telecom Expense Management A comprehensive solution to help you manage the entire mobile and wireline telecommunications lifecycle to achieve substantial savings and enforce compliance by streamlining your telecom business processes and providing complete visibility across your voice, data and mobile telecom assets and infrastructure.

  • Extend and transform

    Strengthen your business capabilities by redefining the end-to-end mobile customer experience. Study your customer with stronger mobile analytics. Open and expand marketplaces. Reach consumers at the moment of decision by leveraging location services, and create value through mobile capabilities that drive loyalty and satisfaction. You can also bring your employees and partners the same benefits with efficient enterprise tools that ease the barriers to mobile collaboration and help improve responses to emerging opportunities.

  • IBM mobile solutions for social business — Enhanced Provide ways for your organization to engage the right people anywhere and enable your employees to be innovators, and customers to become advocates.
  • IBM Mobile Commerce Extends the customer touchpoint to mobile for browsing your online catalog, conducting side-by-side product comparisons, receiving personalized marketing and promotions, completing transactions, obtaining store location information, and providing an accurate view of orders and inventory availability — while seamlessly leveraging the power of personalized marketing and extending the power and reach of your brand. With mobile and tablet starter stores, combined with location-based marketing, you can personalize the mobile shopping experience like never before.
  • IBM WebSphere Portal solutions Provide integrated, personalized multichannel Web experiences through responsive themes that render navigation and page layout on leading devices.
  • IBM Business Process Management solutions Provides the ability to extend your core business processes into your mobile strategy, giving you visibility and control to take your business with you wherever you go.

Mobile services

  • As you approach mobile from a project and strategic perspective, IBM has expertise and resources to complement your own skills where you need them. We can help you understand business in motion and how mobile can positively impact your industry, business strategy, plans and processes. From a development and infrastructure perspective, skills, best practices and expertise are available to assist you in building, connecting, managing and securing your mobile environment, including making sure your existing infrastructure and network can support new mobile demands.

  • IBM Mobile Consulting and Solutions — Enhanced Construct your business-based enterprise mobile strategy and rapidly develop mobile apps based on customer value, technical feasibility and time-to-market priorities using the latest tools and technologies.
  • IBM Mobile Enterprise Services for Managed Mobility — New Manage smartphones, tablets and ruggedized devices across your global enterprise.
  • IBM Mobile Infrastructure Strategy and Planning — Enhanced Accelerate deployment of your mobile initiatives with strategic planning, assessment, and consulting services.
  • IBM Mobile Application Platform Management — New Develop, deploy and manage end-user mobile applications.
  • IBM Network Infrastructure Services for Mobile Prepare wireless and wired networks for secure, high-performance mobile communications with strategy, optimization, integration and managed services.
  • IBM Mobile Security Services Protect smartphone and tablet users against mobile device risks such as theft, viruses, spyware and unauthorized applications.
